Developmental Milestones

Road to Kindergarten

A research-backed checklist of the social, cognitive, language, physical, and self-care skills children typically develop between ages 2 and 5. Track your child's progress at your own pace.

These milestones reflect what 75% or more of children can do by a given age per the CDC's revised 2022 standards, combined with kindergarten readiness checklists from educators. Every child develops at their own pace. Talk to your pediatrician with any concerns.

This checklist is for informational purposes only and is not a substitute for professional developmental screening. If you have concerns about your child's development, contact your pediatrician. You can also download the free CDC Milestone Tracker app.
